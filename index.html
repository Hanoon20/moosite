Our Farm Story

From highland pastures to your table

MooFresh began with a simple belief — that goat milk, done right, could be Sri Lanka's most trusted everyday luxury. We work with small highland farms where goats graze freely on open pasture, exactly as nature intended.

Every bottle is a promise kept between our farmers, our goats, and your family.

Sourcing

Highland Pastures

Goats graze freely across open highland farmland, free of hormones and confinement.

Milking

Gentle, Daily Care

Milked each morning by hand-trained farmers who treat every animal with respect.

Bottling

Fresh Within Hours

Chilled and bottled the same day to lock in freshness and nutrition.
